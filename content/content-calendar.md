## Per-cycle workflow
1. Stay current — research your space; append to `content/research-log.md`.
2. Refresh context — re-read `company-context/*`; update if the company changed.
3. Draft the next queued piece to `content/drafts/`, generate `.docx` (`tools/md-to-docx.py`).
4. Deliver — email the draft to the founder for approval.
5. Update this calendar (delivered + next target date).
